# Changelog: v0.0.151 to v0.0.200

## v0.0.151 - 2026-04-24

`copyreg`: `dispatch_table`, `pickle`, `constructor`. Used by `pickle` to
register reduction functions for extension types.

## v0.0.152 - 2026-04-24

`shelve`: `open`, `Shelf`, `DbfilenameShelf`, `BsdDbShelf`. Key-based
persistent storage backed by `dbm`.

## v0.0.153 - 2026-04-24

`marshal`: `dumps`, `loads`, `dump`, `load`. All CPython 3.14 marshal codes
including tuples, frozensets, small ints, and reference tables.

## v0.0.154 - 2026-04-24

`dbm`: `open`, `whichdb`, `ndbm`/`gdbm`/`dumb` backends via Go's
`github.com/syndtr/goleveldb` store.

## v0.0.155 - 2026-04-24

`sqlite3`: `connect`, `Connection`, `Cursor`, `Row`. DDL, DML, `executemany`,
`executescript`, `row_factory`, `detect_types`, converters/adapters,
`text_factory`, `isolation_level`.

## v0.0.156 - 2026-04-24

`zlib` full coverage: `compress`, `decompress`, `compressobj`, `decompressobj`,
`Compress`/`Decompress` objects with `flush`, `copy`. `crc32`, `adler32`.
Compression levels, window bits, mem level.

## v0.0.157 - 2026-04-24

`gzip`: `open`, `GzipFile`, `compress`, `decompress`. Read/write modes,
compresslevel, mtime, filename in header.

## v0.0.158 - 2026-04-24

`bz2`: `BZ2File`, `BZ2Compressor`, `BZ2Decompressor`, `compress`, `decompress`.
Pure-Go `dsnet/compress` backend.

## v0.0.159 - 2026-04-24

`lzma`: `LZMAFile`, `LZMACompressor`, `LZMADecompressor`, `compress`,
`decompress`. FORMAT_XZ, FORMAT_ALONE, FORMAT_RAW, CHECK_CRC64. Pure-Go
`ulikunitz/xz` backend.

## v0.0.160 - 2026-04-24

`zipfile`: `ZipFile`, `ZipInfo`, `is_zipfile`, `Path`. Read, write, append
modes. `ZIP_STORED`, `ZIP_DEFLATED`, `ZIP_BZIP2`, `ZIP_LZMA` methods.

## v0.0.161 - 2026-04-24

`tarfile`: `open`, `TarFile`, `TarInfo`. Read/write/append modes.
`gzopen`, `bz2open`, `xzopen`. `getmember`, `getmembers`, `extractall`,
`add`, `addfile`, PAX format.

## v0.0.162 - 2026-04-24

`zstd` (`compression.zstd`): `compress`, `decompress`, `ZstdCompressor`,
`ZstdDecompressor`, `ZstdDict`. Pure-Go `klauspost/compress/zstd` backend.

## v0.0.163 - 2026-04-24

`csv` full coverage: `reader`, `writer`, `DictReader`, `DictWriter`,
`register_dialect`, `unregister_dialect`, `list_dialects`, `Dialect`, `excel`,
`excel_tab`, `unix_dialect`. All `quoting` constants.

## v0.0.164 - 2026-04-24

`configparser`: `ConfigParser`, `RawConfigParser`, `SafeConfigParser`,
`SectionProxy`. Interpolation (`BasicInterpolation`, `ExtendedInterpolation`),
`read`, `write`, `items`, `options`.

## v0.0.165 - 2026-04-24

`tomllib`: TOML v1.0 parser. Tables, arrays of tables, inline tables,
multi-line strings, literal strings, dates, times, datetimes, hex/oct/bin
integers, `+inf`, `-inf`, `nan`.

## v0.0.166 - 2026-04-24

`netrc`: `netrc`, `netrc_entry`. Parses `.netrc` format; `authenticators`,
`hosts`, `macros`.

## v0.0.167 - 2026-04-24

`plistlib`: `loads`, `dumps`, `load`, `dump`. XML and binary plist formats.
`FMT_XML`, `FMT_BINARY`. `UID`, `Data`. All Python types round-trip.

## v0.0.168 - 2026-04-24

`hashlib` full coverage: `md5`, `sha1`, `sha224`, `sha256`, `sha384`,
`sha512`, `sha3_224`, `sha3_256`, `sha3_384`, `sha3_512`, `shake_128`,
`shake_256`, `blake2b`, `blake2s`. `algorithms_available`, `new`.

## v0.0.169 - 2026-04-24

`hmac` full coverage: SHA-3 digestmod, `block_size`, `copy`, callable
digestmod, `compare_digest`.

## v0.0.170 - 2026-04-24

`secrets` full coverage: correct error messages, `choice`, `randbits`,
`token_bytes`, `token_hex`, `token_urlsafe`, `compare_digest`.

## v0.0.171 - 2026-04-24

`os` full coverage: `walk`, `scandir` (with `DirEntry`), file-descriptor
operations (`open`, `read`, `write`, `close`, `dup`, `dup2`, `fstat`, `ftruncate`),
`environ` improvements, `os.O_*` constants, `os.cpu_count`.

## v0.0.172 - 2026-04-24

`io` full coverage: `BytesIO` all methods, `StringIO` all methods,
`FileIO` seek/tell/iter, `TextIOWrapper`, `BufferedReader`/`Writer`/
`Random`, `DEFAULT_BUFFER_SIZE`, `SEEK_SET`/`CUR`/`END`.

## v0.0.173 - 2026-04-24

`time` full coverage: `struct_time`, `strftime`, `strptime`, `localtime`,
`gmtime`, `mktime`, `monotonic`, `perf_counter`, `process_time`, `clock_gettime`,
`CLOCK_REALTIME`, `CLOCK_MONOTONIC`, `CLOCK_PROCESS_CPUTIME_ID`.

## v0.0.174 - 2026-04-24

`logging` full coverage: `Logger`, `Handler`, `Formatter`, `Filter`,
`LogRecord`. `StreamHandler`, `FileHandler`, `NullHandler`. Level constants,
`basicConfig`, `getLogger`, `disable`, `captureWarnings`.

## v0.0.175 - 2026-04-24

`logging.config`: `dictConfig`, `fileConfig`, `listen`, `stopListening`.
Schema validation and incremental updates.

## v0.0.176 - 2026-04-24

`logging.handlers`: `RotatingFileHandler`, `TimedRotatingFileHandler`,
`SocketHandler`, `DatagramHandler`, `SysLogHandler`, `HTTPHandler`,
`MemoryHandler`, `QueueHandler`, `QueueListener`.

## v0.0.177 - 2026-04-24

`platform`: `system`, `node`, `release`, `version`, `machine`, `processor`,
`architecture`, `python_version`, `python_implementation`, `uname`, `uname_result`.

## v0.0.178 - 2026-04-24

`errno`: all standard POSIX error constants (`EPERM`, `ENOENT`, `EACCES`,
`EAGAIN`, `EWOULDBLOCK`, `EINPROGRESS`, `ENOTCONN`, `EADDRINUSE`, etc.)
with their numeric values.

## v0.0.179 - 2026-04-24

`ctypes`: `c_int`, `c_uint`, `c_long`, `c_ulong`, `c_char`, `c_char_p`,
`c_void_p`, `c_bool`, `c_float`, `c_double`. `POINTER`, `pointer`, `cast`,
`byref`, `addressof`, `sizeof`, `Structure`, `Union`, `Array`.

## v0.0.180 - 2026-04-24

`argparse`: `ArgumentParser`, `add_argument`, `parse_args`, `parse_known_args`.
All action types, `nargs`, `type`, `default`, `required`, `choices`,
`metavar`. Subparsers, argument groups, mutually exclusive groups.

## v0.0.181 - 2026-04-24

`optparse`: `OptionParser`, `add_option`, `parse_args`. `store`, `store_true`,
`store_false`, `store_const`, `append`, `count`, `callback` actions.

## v0.0.182 - 2026-04-24

`getpass`: `getpass`, `getuser`. Falls back to `input` when no TTY is present.

## v0.0.183 - 2026-04-24

`fileinput`: `input`, `filename`, `fileno`, `lineno`, `filelineno`,
`isfirstline`, `isstdin`, `nextfile`, `close`. `FileInput` class.
`hook_compressed`, `hook_encoded`.

## v0.0.184 - 2026-04-24

`curses`: `initscr`, `newwin`, `wrapper`. Window methods: `addstr`, `addch`,
`getch`, `refresh`, `clear`, `border`, `box`, `move`, `getyx`, `getmaxyx`,
`keypad`, `scrollok`, `resize`. `cbreak`, `noecho`, `start_color`, `init_pair`.

## v0.0.185 - 2026-04-24

`curses.textpad`: `Textbox` with all editing commands (Ctrl-A through Ctrl-Z).
`rectangle` helper. All ACS line-drawing constants.

## v0.0.186 - 2026-04-24

`curses.ascii`: all 32 classification functions (`isalpha`, `isdigit`,
`isspace`, etc.), conversion functions (`toascii`, `toupper`, `tolower`),
all named ASCII constants (`NUL`, `SOH`, ... `DEL`).

## v0.0.187 - 2026-04-24

`curses.panel`: `new_panel`, `top_panel`, `bottom_panel`, `update_panels`,
`doupdate`. `Panel` methods: `bottom`, `top`, `above`, `below`, `hidden`,
`show`, `hide`, `window`, `move`, `replace`, `set_userptr`, `userptr`.

## v0.0.188 - 2026-04-24

`cmd`: `Cmd` base class. `cmdloop`, `onecmd`, `parseline`, `emptyline`,
`default`, `do_help`. Prompt, intro, command completion via `readline`.

## v0.0.189 - 2026-04-25

`threading` rewritten with real goroutines. `Thread` maps to `goroutine`.
`Lock`, `RLock`, `Condition`, `Semaphore`, `BoundedSemaphore`, `Event`,
`Barrier` all backed by Go sync primitives. `threading.local` per-goroutine.
No GIL.

## v0.0.190 - 2026-04-25

`multiprocessing`: goroutine-backed `Process`, `Queue`, `Pipe`, `Pool`
worker pool. `Value`, `Array` shared-memory types. `Manager` proxy objects.
`cpu_count`, `active_children`, `current_process`.

## v0.0.191 - 2026-04-25

`multiprocessing.shared_memory`: `SharedMemory`, `ShareableList`.
Global `sync.Map` registry for name-based lookup. `unlink` cleans up.

## v0.0.192 - 2026-04-25

`concurrent.futures`: `ThreadPoolExecutor`, `ProcessPoolExecutor`, `Future`,
`wait`, `as_completed`, `FIRST_COMPLETED`, `FIRST_EXCEPTION`, `ALL_COMPLETED`.
`CancelledError`, `TimeoutError`, `InvalidStateError`.

## v0.0.193 - 2026-04-25

`concurrent.interpreters`: `Interpreter`, `Queue`. `exec`, `call`,
`call_in_thread`, `prepare_main`. Per-interpreter isolated namespaces.

## v0.0.194 - 2026-04-25

`subprocess`: `run`, `Popen`, `call`, `check_call`, `check_output`,
`getoutput`, `getstatusoutput`. `CalledProcessError`, `TimeoutExpired`.
`PIPE`, `DEVNULL`, `STDOUT`. stdin/stdout/stderr wiring.

## v0.0.195 - 2026-04-25

`sched`: `scheduler` backed by `container/heap`. `enter`, `enterabs`,
`cancel`, `empty`, `run`, `queue` property. Priority-based event ordering.

## v0.0.196 - 2026-04-25

`queue`: `Queue`, `LifoQueue`, `PriorityQueue`, `SimpleQueue`. `task_done`,
`join`, `shutdown`. `Empty`, `Full`, `ShutDown` exceptions.

## v0.0.197 - 2026-04-25

`contextvars`: `ContextVar`, `Token`, `Context`, `copy_context`. Per-goroutine
`ctxStack` on `Interp`. Thread isolation via `threadCopy` clone.

## v0.0.198 - 2026-04-25

`_thread`: `start_new_thread`, `allocate_lock`, `get_ident`, `exit`,
`stack_size`. `LockType` with timeout-aware `acquire`.

## v0.0.199 - 2026-04-25

`asyncio` full CPython 3.14 API: `Task`, `Future`, `create_task`,
`ensure_future`, `wait`, `wait_for`, `as_completed`. `Lock`, `Event`,
`Condition`, `Semaphore`, `BoundedSemaphore`. `Queue`, `LifoQueue`,
`PriorityQueue`. `timeout`, `TaskGroup`, `shield`, `to_thread`.
`CancelledError`, `TimeoutError`, `InvalidStateError`.

## v0.0.200 - 2026-04-25

`socket`: TCP/UDP backed by Go `net`. `socketpair` via `syscall.Socketpair`.
`inet_aton`/`ntoa`/`pton`/`ntop`. `htons`, `ntohs`, `htonl`, `ntohl`.
`getaddrinfo`, `gethostname`, `gethostbyname`, `gethostbyaddr`.
`getprotobyname`, `getservbyname`. Full `AF_*`, `SOCK_*`, `IPPROTO_*`,
`SO_*` constants.
